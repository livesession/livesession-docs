# User paths
<Subtitle>
    Learn how to track user paths.
</Subtitle>

**User paths** enable you to find the journey of your users on your app.
You can search the exact steps they took to reach a specific page or perform a specific action.
This can help you understand how users interact with your app which gives you
unlimited abilities like identifying potential issues or areas for improvement.
